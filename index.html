About the Owner – Pratibha Pathak

Mrs. Pratibha Pathak, the proud owner and founder of IPS Salon, has been redefining beauty and confidence since 2008. With years of dedication and expertise in the beauty and wellness industry, she has built IPS Salon into one of Sasaram’s most trusted and loved female salons.

Her passion for empowering women through professional beauty care and her commitment to maintaining high standards of hygiene, creativity, and customer satisfaction have made IPS Salon a preferred destination for modern women in Sasaram Rohtas, Bihar.

Under her guidance, the salon continues to offer top-notch hair, skin, makeup, and nail services , blending traditional care with the latest trends and techniques.
